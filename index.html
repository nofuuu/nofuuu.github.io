Goblin Slayer-Slayer:
A hybrid card game

A hybrid card game made in a team of seven during University of Jyväskylä 2021-2022 hybrid game course (featuring physical aspects of the game made in collaboration with Gradia students).

My job was to plan and create the art for the application and the goblin playing cards.

Check the project on GitHub. Playing cards are included as a pdf file.

2021

Bad Feather Day:
A 3D platforming game

This was my first proper experience working on a game in a team. This game was made in a team of seven during University of Jyväskylä 2021 game course.

My job was to plan and create the art for the game. This was my first time with animation and creating 3d graphics. I chose a low poly style to keep the scope of the project feasible and made the models using Blender.
